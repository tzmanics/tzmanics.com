DESIGN

Personal Project: MANICS Font Type

Tools: Illustrator, Baltic Birch Plywood, Vandercook No.4 Press

I created MANICS Font manipulating a few simple shapes in Illustrator. Using a blade and a 2/2 hand skew gouge, I carved the type into baltic birch plywood and cut out the type blocks. I then printed the blocks with a rainbow roller at Bow & Arrow Press.

Personal Project: Adjustable Cigar Ashtray

Tools: Wood, hand tools and imagination

This was created for my father who hand carves beautiful ashtrays in his free time. I wanted a design that never let the cigar tip over onto it's foot into the ash, as I've seen so often with other ashtrays. The ash collector is stable while the 'headrest' slides up and down the wooden dowel to match the cigar's length.
